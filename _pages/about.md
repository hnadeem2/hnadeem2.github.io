I am a Ph.D. student in Bioengineering at the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign and a member of the [Shukla group](https://shuklagroup.org/).

My research focuses on computational approaches for biomolecular systems, including enhanced sampling, protein structural ensembles, protein language model representations, molecular charge transport in peptides, and molecular design principles for bioinspired materials.

Avid fan of [Liverpool FC](https://www.liverpoolfc.com/). Have more experience handling cows, goats and other farm animals than proteins. Always happy to chat (if food is involved).
